About Me

While I focus on my studies, creating high-quality video content across various niches including technology, explainer videos, and insightful tutorials is my exciting side hustle. At 'Gokul Narrates', I explore the power of storytelling, producing engaging narratives that captivate and inspire audiences. Join me on this wonderful journey of exploration and creativity. Subscribe to 'Gokul Narrates' and 'Mr. Technetium' for your regular dose of innovation and inspiration, crafted by a kid who loves to learn and create!
